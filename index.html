Human Hormone Index

\ \
\ \
\ \
\

Adrenaline

\

Produced by: Adrenal Medulla

\

Function: Fight-or-flight response

\

Activated by: Stress

\

Effects of Inactivation: Low energy during stress

\
\ \
\

Insulin

\

Produced by: Pancreas

\

Function: Regulates blood sugar levels

\

Activated by: High blood sugar

\

Effects of Inactivation: High blood sugar (Diabetes)

\
\ \
\

Thyroxine

\

Produced by: Thyroid Gland

\

Function: Regulates metabolism

\

Activated by: TSH (Thyroid Stimulating Hormone)

\

Effects of Inactivation: Slow metabolism, weight gain
